About the Game

Summer Scholars was created under the University of Delaware's Summer Scholars program. I used this time for research on Technical Art, focusing on rigging, shaders, and workflows. I also kept a very well detailed log of documentation of my inspirations, references, as well as difficulties throughout the whole process. You can access my documentation right here.

Play the Game - It's on itch because the size is relatively larger than my other games.

Development Notes

  • Built in Godot
  • 3 Months Development Time
  • Aside from terrain textures, I made everything in it!
  • This includes meshing, texturing, rigging, and programming the main character.
  • This also includes shaders, particles... essentially everything visual.
